Our Introduction

The Hutter-Schenider Robotics Club is a massive community of tech nerds who understand and learn the art of robotics and machinery. It was started by Ralf Hutter of Kraftwerk as a company for people who are into robotics, solely dedicated to the late Florian Schneider, a close friend of Ralf. The concept was a reference to Kraftwerk's 1978 hit album, "The Man-Machine." Innovation is always key to making really cool pieces of machinery we call, "robots".
